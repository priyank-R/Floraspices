Welcome to Flora Spices

FLORA TRADING was found in 2003, and it has been 17 years building strong relationships with the local farmers - manufacturing spice products for exporting, distributing and exporting tropical products special products of India. Seeing the importance of spices and herbs, Flora Trading decided to be expert in expanding exporting spice products under the brand of FLORA SPICES.

Our company has over the years positioned itself in a highly competitive market with our business strategy, high quality standards, cost-effectiveness, sound business policies and prompt delivery. As a result, we are well known as a manufacturer and exporter of safe and clean spices in India. Our products have been exported all over the world:

  • USA, China, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Singapore, Russia, India, Malaysia
  • Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Turkey
  • South Africa, Algeria
  • Britain, France, Germany
  • United States, Uruguay, Paraguay
  • Australia
